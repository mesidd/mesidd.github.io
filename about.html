About Me

AI Systems Architect & First Principle Thinker

I believe the future of AI will be shaped not by brute-force scale, but by architectural innovation—systems designed to reason, adapt, and learn efficiently.

The current focus on ever-larger models and energy-intensive training delivers power, but at immense cost and diminishing returns.

My work takes a different path: building elegant, energy-efficient algorithmic frameworks capable of tackling complex, NP-hard problems through intelligent design rather than raw compute.

True progress toward AGI will emerge from architectures that mirror the efficiency and adaptability of natural cognition—machines that think by design, not by scale.
