Architecting Machine Cognition

My work is built on a foundation of cognitive science, logic, and first-principles thinking to create systems that truly understand.

The Foundational Work: "The Living Brain"

The core of my research is “The Living Brain: A Trilogy of Cognition.” This isn't just a paper; it's a working Neuro-symbolic framework designed from the ground up to reason, synthesize, and create. It moves beyond statistical pattern matching to build a system with a cohesive model of the world.

The 12-Stage Cognitive Architecture

This framework is built upon a novel 12-stage cognitive architecture. Each stage represents a distinct cognitive function, from raw perception and sensory fusion to abstract reasoning and long-term planning. This modular, yet deeply integrated, design allows for inspectable, robust, and adaptable intelligence.
